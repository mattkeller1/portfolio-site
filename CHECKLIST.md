## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Leaving "Your Name" or placeholder text
❌ Broken social media links
❌ Case studies with fake data
❌ Not testing on mobile
❌ Deploying before proofreading
❌ Using example.com email addresses

✅ Real information throughout
✅ Working links
✅ Actual case study results
✅ Mobile-friendly
✅ Professional polish
✅ Your real contact info
